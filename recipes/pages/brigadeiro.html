Brigadeiro

Ingredients

  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powde
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Chocolate sprinkles or shredded coconut for coating

Instructions

  1. In a non-stick pan, combine sweetened condensed milk, cocoa powder, and butter.
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens and starts to pull away from the sides of the pan (about 10-15 minutes).
  3. Remove from heat and let it cool to room temperature
  4. Grease your hands with butter and roll the mixture into small balls.
  5. Roll the balls in chocolate sprinkles or shredded coconut to coat.
  6. Place the brigadeiros in small paper cups or on a plate.
  7. Chill in the refrigerator for a couple of hours before serving.
