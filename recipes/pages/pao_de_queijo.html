Ingredients

  • 2 cups tapioca flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 beaten eggs

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375째F (190째C) and grease a mini muffin tin.
  2. n a saucepan, combine the milk, butter, and salt. Heat over medium heat until it starts to boil, then remove from heat.
  3. Add the tapioca flour to the hot milk mixture and stir until well combined. Let it cool for a few minutes.
  4. Once the mixture has cooled slightly, add the beaten eggs and mix well.
  5. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until the dough is smooth.
  6. Spoon the dough into the mini muffin tin, filling each cup almost to the top.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for about 15-20 minutes or until the tops are lightly golden.
  8. Remove from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.
