pão de queijo

Ingredients

  • 2 cups tapioca flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 beaten eggs

Instructions

  1. Place beans in a large pot with enough water to cover, and soak overnight.
  2. Drain beans, and return to the pot with fresh water to cover. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer 1 hour, or until beans are tender. Drain, and set aside.
  3. Place the pork shoulder, chorizo, smoked sausage, and bacon in a large pot over medium heat. Cook 15 minutes, or until well browned. Remove from heat, drain, and set aside.
  4. Place the onion in the pot, and cook until tender. Return pork products to the pot, and mix in the garlic. Cook and stir until garlic is tender. Mix in the bay leaves, and season with salt and pepper. Continue to cook and stir until mixture is well blended.
  5. Stir the beans into the pot with the pork mixture. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, and simmer 2 hours, stirring occasionally, until beans are tender.
  6. Remove bay leaves before serving.

Eat with coffe, my conrad.
